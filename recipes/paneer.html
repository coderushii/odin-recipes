Paneer Recipe

Home

Description

Paneer is a significant part of Indian cuisine. It is a fresh, soft Indian cheese that is used in a variety of dishes.

Ingredients

  • 500g paneer, cut into 1/2 inch cubes
  • 3 tablespoons oil
  • 4 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 4 medium tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 tablespoon ground cashews
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon salt, or to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds.
  2. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  4. Add pureed tomatoes and cook until oil separates.
  5. Mix in ground cashews, garam masala, ground coriander, and salt.
  6. Add paneer cubes and gently mix to coat.
  7. Cook for a few minutes, then serve hot.

Enjoy your homemade paneer dish!
