Chicken Biryani Recipe

Home

Description

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic Indian dish made with layers of marinated chicken, fragrant basmati rice, and a blend of spices. It is often garnished with fried onions, fresh cilantro, and mint leaves.

Ingredients

  • 500g basmati rice
  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint leaves
  • 3 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 4 cups water
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Rinse the basmati rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Soak the rice in water for 30 minutes, then drain.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the chicken pieces with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, ground turmeric, ground coriander, garam masala, red chili powder, and salt. Marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Heat ghee or oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them sizzle.
  4. Add sliced onions and saut√© until golden brown. Remove half of the onions and set aside for garnish.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes to the pot and cook until they soften.
  6. Add the marinated chicken to the pot and cook until the chicken is no longer pink.
  7. In a separate pot, bring 4 cups of water to a boil. Add the soaked and drained rice, and cook until the rice is 70% cooked. Drain the rice.
  8. Layer the partially cooked rice over the chicken in the pot. Sprinkle chopped cilantro and mint leaves on top.
  9. Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld and the rice to finish cooking.
  10. Remove from heat and let it rest for 10 minutes before serving. Garnish with the reserved fried onions.
  11. Serve hot with raita or a side salad.

Enjoy your homemade chicken biryani!
