Butter Chicken Recipe

Home

Description

Butter chicken, also known as murgh makhani, is a popular Indian dish made with chicken cooked in a spiced tomato, butter, and cream sauce. It is known for its rich and creamy texture and is often served with naan or rice.

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 medium tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat butter in a pan and add cumin seeds.
  2. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  4. Add pureed tomatoes and cook until oil separates.
  5. Mix in ground turmeric, ground coriander, garam masala, and salt.
  6. Add chicken pieces and cook until they are no longer pink.
  7. Pour in heavy cream and simmer for a few minutes until the sauce thickens.
  8. Garnish with fresh cilantro if desired and serve hot.

Enjoy your homemade butter chicken!
